Gru Stag Hunt

The study of evolutionary games has attracted great interest on the problem of emergence of cooperation. In this talk, I will go through the well-studied framework, the Prisoner’s Dilemma, to illustrate Nash Equilibrium. I will, then, proceed to discuss a “coordination” game, Stag Hunt, in order to draw a parallel with hierarchical organization of strategies according to the cooperation in the network. Depending on the strategy dynamics, I will try to find a possible transition from a non-cooperation to a cooperation state.

May 15, 2019
Steve Baker Plate A Sheet

Philately is really a large complex building, but most who enter, do not proceed much beyond the huge foyer. A small number of people focus their attention on some of the smaller rooms, while others explore the corridors connecting all rooms. Just a handful of philatelists explore a massive problem, using a single stamp. This mania demands adherence to strict scientific principles of investigation, requiring attention and resolve, bordering on insanity. And for what reward? Nothing, except the delight in solving a puzzle.

 June 12, 2019
Emily Douwes Body Talk

When we get stressed out and an illness knocks us out, we generally take it as a sign to slow down, rest, and listen to our body. This is just one way that our bodies speak to us. Another way our bodies speak to us is through chronic pain or conditions. Depending on which side of the body, which body part, and the severity of the pain, we can gather clues about an unresolved situation or emotion that may be the root cause.Let's talk about these connections and how alternative healing methods such as hypnotherapy and energy healing can provide answers and relief.

 July 10, 2019
Tony Stecca Knots for Neckties

A review of four common knots for neckties, including the Full Windsor, Half Windsor, Four-in-hand, and the Nicki (Pratt) Knot. Attendees will learn to tie each knot, along with the history and utility of each knot. This is a hands-on, interactive talk. Materials provided.

 August 20, 2019
